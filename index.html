About Om Aasha Hospital

A trusted multi-specialty hospital dedicated to the health and wellbeing of Dhangadhi and greater Kailali.

Our Story

Built to bring quality healthcare closer to home.

Om Aasha Hospital Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a simple purpose: to give the people of Dhangadhi and the wider Sudurpashchim Province access to reliable, modern, and compassionate healthcare without needing to travel far. Located on Chowk Campus Road, Ward No. 8, the hospital has grown into a multi-specialty centre offering emergency, inpatient, diagnostic, and outpatient services around the clock.

Affiliated with APHIN (Association of Private Health Institutions Nepal) and rated 4.7/5 by patients, Om Aasha continues to invest in qualified specialists, modern equipment, and a warm patient experience.
